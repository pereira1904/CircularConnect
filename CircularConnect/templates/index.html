About the Summit

The Circular Technology Summit brings together professionals from across Europe and beyond to explore how technology can drive the circular economy. Over two days, attendees will engage with cutting-edge research, practical workshops, and thought-provoking panel discussions.

Key Topics:

  • Data-centre hardware reuse and recycling
  • Reverse logistics and circular supply chains
  • Sustainable technology and green computing
  • Digital product passports
  • AI in circular supply chains
  • Secure data erasure and responsible disposal
